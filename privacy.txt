Privacy Notice – Shoutout

Last Updated: 6 October 2025

At Shoutout, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Account Information: Name, email, profile details.

Transaction Data: Billing info if you purchase a subscription.

Usage Data: Pages visited, links clicked, device type, IP address.

Communication Data: Messages, feedback, or survey responses.

2. How We Use Your Information

Provide and improve Shoutout Services.

Process payments and manage subscriptions.

Personalize your experience and deliver relevant content.

Detect fraud and ensure security.

Marketing and promotions (with consent).

3. Sharing Your Information

With service providers to deliver our Services.

With affiliate and partner programs if you opt-in.

When required by law or to protect rights and safety.

With your consent or at your direction.

4. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to countries with different data protection laws. We implement safeguards to protect your data.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies. See our Cookie Notice for details.

6. Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for our services or legal obligations.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

Access, correct, or delete personal information.

Restrict processing or object to certain uses.

Opt-out of marketing communications.

Withdraw consent where applicable.

8. Children

Shoutout is not for children under 18. We delete data if we learn it was collected from a child.

9. Changes to This Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice. The “last updated” date shows the most recent revision.
